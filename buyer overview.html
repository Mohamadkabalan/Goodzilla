WHAT’S IN IT FOR YOU?

As a buyer, you want to make sure you’re getting the best deal out there. That’s why, when you use Goodzillas, you can get quotations from different sources from the comfort of your own home. You can even request a (paid) sample to make sure it’s up to your standards before placing your order. Once you’ve placed your order and paid for it, a customer service agent is assigned to follow your shipment until you confirm that you’ve received it. All this is part of our trade insurance: the supplier only receives your money once you’ve received your order and are satisfied with it. The best part: you get all this without having to pay commission!
